=== TITLE ===
Server Guardian Warboard - OpenAI Build Week Demo

=== DESCRIPTION ===
Someone kept trying to break into my personal server. Server Guardian Warboard
turns the privacy-filtered signals from a read-only decoy into RPG, map, NORAD,
battle, and first-person views without exposing the system it protects.

Video: https://youtu.be/J-gfPQeM5WA
Demo: https://plzhacknono.duckdns.org/
Source: https://github.com/yazzang-homelab/server-guardian-warboard

Built with Codex and GPT-5.6 for OpenAI Build Week (Developer Tools track).
During the Submission Period, Codex and GPT-5.6 assisted with privacy-boundary
hardening, complete English/Korean judge coverage, deterministic demo scenes,
six-mode QA, and submission documentation. Product direction, safety and privacy
boundaries, visual language, and final acceptance remained human decisions.

All application footage was captured from the live public product. The ten-second
cyber-defense cold open was generated with Higgsfield from a privacy-safe project
frame and includes synchronized generated effects. English narration was
synthesized with ElevenLabs. The underscore and transition effects were
synthesized specifically for this video by the entrant; no stock or copyrighted
music is used.
